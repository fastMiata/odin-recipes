Ramen

Ingredients

  • Pork Center Loin
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Bay Leaves
  • Peppercorn

Directions

  1. In a bowl marinade cubed pork loins with garlic and soy sauce. Cover with plastic wrap and allow to sit for 1-2 hours
  2. Bring a pan to medium-high heat. Add a little bit of canola oil and saute garlic-beef mixture until sides are browned. Add peppercorn and bay leaves.
  3. Add two cups of water to pan and bring to a boil. Cover with a lid and allow the mixture to simmer for 40min-60min
  4. Add more water if pan is dry. Add half a cup of vinegar and let simmer for 15 minutes without stirring!. (Let vinegar mixture settle and top do not stir)
  5. Serve over rice
